Ogbono Soup

Description

Ogbono soup is a soup from the south eastern geographical region of Nigeria. It can be eaten with Eba

Ingredients

  • Ogbono seed.
  • Palm oil.
  • Chopped Ugu leaves(flute pumpkin leaves).
  • Condiments: seasoning cubes, ground shrimps.
  • Powdered chili pepper.
  • Meat: beef,chevon, chicken, ponmo(cow skin).
  • Fishes:Smoked catfish, dried and de-oiled red herring,iced mackerel

Steps

  1. Cook the meat and fishes with salt and condiments until tender for about 45 minutes. Drain the stock from the content so you can use it to flavor the ogbono soup.
  2. In another pot on low heat, add some palm oil and add the Powdered ogbono seed to it and allow the palm oil to heat up
  3. Mix thoroughly with palm oil till the ogbono seeds are completely coated.
  4. Add the stock and water respectively, and stir until it begins to thicken and the texture becomes viscous in nature. When that is achieved, ogbono soup completion process is close to maturity.
  5. You can add a bit more stock or water until desired viscosity is achieved.
  6. Once the desired viscosity is attained, bring to boil, then set to low heat and let it cook for 10 minutes and stir intermittently.
  7. Add the cooked meat and fishes, Also add the chili pepper with salt to taste
  8. If it becomes too thick, add a little more water. Let it simmer for 10 more minutes.
  9. Add the chopped ugu leaves and let simmer on low heat for about 5 minutes
  10. Ogbono Soup is ready to be served
